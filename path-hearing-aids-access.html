NHS Access in Bristol

Provided free as a long-term loan via UHBW Audiology (St Michael’s, Southmead, and satellite clinics). Lost/damaged device fee: £95.

Private Supply

Local providers include Audika, Hidden Hearing, Boots Hearingcare, The Hearing Care Partnership, and Gordon Morris.

Contact UHBW Audiology (St Michael's Hospital)

Phone: 0117 342 5854 (Note: Phone lines can be very busy)

Email: audiology.dept@uhbw.nhs.uk

Free Replacement Tubing: Once you have your hearing aid, you do not need to buy replacement tubes. You can email the audiology department to request new tubes, and they will send them to you for free by post.

Battery Supply: If your hearing aid uses disposable batteries, replacements can also be requested via the NHS.

Hearing Aid Maintenance

Knowing how to keep a hearing aid working could be the difference between comfortable hearing and social isolation. Maintenance includes:

  • Changing the batteries
  • Keeping the earmoulds clear
  • Retubing earmoulds
  • Replacing thintubes
