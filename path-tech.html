Auracast & Next-Gen Accessibility

Auracast™ broadcast audio is a new Bluetooth® LE Audio standard that allows a single transmitter to broadcast high-quality, low-latency audio to an unlimited number of receivers simultaneously.

Bristol Temple Meads Deployment

Bristol Temple Meads is the site of the world's first public rail station trial of Auracast, providing real-time PA announcements, delays, and safety alerts directly to passengers' hearing devices.

Key Partners

  • Centre for Deaf and Hard of Hearing People (CfD Bristol)
  • Connected Places Catapult (Station Innovation Zone)
  • RNID (User feedback studies)

UK Venue Adoption

Major venues across the UK, such as The National Theatre, Shakespeare’s Globe, and the Southbank Centre, are committed to integrating Auracast infrastructure.
