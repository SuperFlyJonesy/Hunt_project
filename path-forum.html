Our Patreon Forum

To ensure our community remains a completely safe, troll-free, and supportive space, we host our private discussions on Patreon.

Why do we ask for your Name and Postcode?

  • Your Name & Email: Used exclusively to verify your identity and send your private invite link to the Patreon forum.
  • Your Postcode: Helps us map where our community is located. This allows us to book venues for coffee meetups exactly where they are needed most, and provides crucial data to help secure NHS and Council funding for specific Bristol postcodes.
