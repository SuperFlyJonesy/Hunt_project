About Us

Hi, we are Jason and Jo. As former CFD employees and proud members of the Hard of Hearing (HoH) community, we understand firsthand the barriers and isolation that can come with hearing loss. We built this initiative to create a safe, accessible, and supportive space for everyone.

Right now, this is a grassroots project. Every donation we receive goes directly into the community. Your support pays for accessible venue hire, ensures everyone gets a free coffee at our meetups, and helps us bring in expert guest speakers. This community-funded model bridges the gap while we work towards securing long-term backing from corporate sponsors and the local council.
