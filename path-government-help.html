Government Help & Support

Personal Independence Payment (PIP)

Personal Independence Payment (PIP) is a non-means-tested, tax-free UK government benefit designed to help with extra living costs if you have a long-term physical or mental health condition or disability.

Who Qualifies & Eligible Hearing Conditions

For those with hearing loss, qualifying issues include:

  • Severe or Profound Deafness.
  • Significant Hearing Impairment.
  • Associated conditions: Chronic tinnitus, balance/vestibular disorders, lasting physical effects of ear surgeries.

Access to Work Grant

Access to Work is a DWP grant scheme providing practical and financial support if your health condition or disability affects your ability to do your job.

What It Covers

  • Specialist Hearing Aids & Custom Molds.
  • Assistive Listening Devices (e.g., remote microphones).
  • Connectivity Solutions (Bluetooth streamers).
  • Communication Support (BSL interpreters, note-takers).

Interaction with Government State Pension

If you already receive PIP, your payments will continue as normal upon reaching State Pension age. Access to Work grants do not affect your State Pension or other benefits.
